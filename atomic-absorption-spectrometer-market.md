<p><p>The Atomic Absorption Spectrometer market is segmented into three main types. The Flame Atomic Absorption Spectrometer uses a flame to vaporize samples, making it suitable for high-concentration analysis. The Graphite Furnace Atomic Absorption Spectrometer provides higher sensitivity and can analyze trace elements due to its ability to heat samples in a graphite platform. The Integrated Atomic Absorption Spectrometer combines both flame and graphite furnace techniques, offering versatility and efficiency for various analytical applications in laboratories and industries.</p></p>
