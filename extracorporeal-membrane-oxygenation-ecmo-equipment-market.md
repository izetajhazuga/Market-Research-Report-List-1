<p><strong>The Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation ECMO Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>VA (Veno-Arterial) ECMO Equipment</li><li>VV (Veno-Venous) ECMO Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation (ECMO) equipment market is divided into two main types: Veno-Arterial (VA) ECMO and Veno-Venous (VV) ECMO. VA ECMO is designed to support patients with severe cardiac and pulmonary issues by providing both cardiac and respiratory assistance, as it circulates blood from a vein to an artery. In contrast, VV ECMO focuses solely on supporting patients with respiratory failure, allowing blood to flow between veins without providing cardiac support. Each type serves specific clinical needs in critical care settings.</p></p>
