<p><ul><li>Static Surface Tension Meter</li><li>Dynamic Surface Tension Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surface tension meters are categorized into static and dynamic types. Static surface tension meters measure the tension of a liquid's surface when it is at rest, providing a direct reading of the liquid's cohesive properties. Dynamic surface tension meters, on the other hand, assess the surface tension during processes like bubble formation or liquid flow, capturing the changing interactions of the liquid with air or other materials. Both types are essential for various applications, including quality control in industries like food and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
