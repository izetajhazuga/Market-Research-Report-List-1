<p><p>The 5G wireless equipment market comprises various base station types catering to different coverage and capacity needs. Macro base stations (BS) offer widespread coverage for urban and rural areas, while micro BS focus on enhancing capacity in densely populated regions. Pico BS serve smaller, localized areas, providing targeted service in venues like malls or offices. Femto BS are designed for home use, enabling improved indoor connectivity. Each type plays a crucial role in supporting the diverse requirements of 5G networks.</p></p>
