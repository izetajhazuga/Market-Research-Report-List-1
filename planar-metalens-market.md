<p><p>The planar metalens market includes three primary types: spherical lenses, metal lenses, and others. Spherical lenses, traditionally made from glass or plastic, have a curved surface to focus light. Metal lenses, utilizing nanostructured materials, manipulate light at subwavelength scales, offering advantages like reduced weight and thickness. The "others" category encompasses alternative designs and technologies, such as gradient index lenses and photonic devices, which provide unique optical properties and applications in areas like imaging, sensing, and communications.</p></p>
