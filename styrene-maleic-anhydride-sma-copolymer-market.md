<p><p>Styrene Maleic Anhydride (SMA) copolymer market is primarily divided into two types: A-SMA and R-SMA. A-SMA refers to the amorphous form, characterized by its flexibility and compatibility with various resins, making it suitable for applications like adhesives and coatings. R-SMA, on the other hand, is a rubber-like variant that offers enhanced toughness and impact resistance, ideal for automotive and industrial applications. Both types serve diverse industries, contributing to the growth and versatility of SMA copolymers in various formulations.</p></p>
