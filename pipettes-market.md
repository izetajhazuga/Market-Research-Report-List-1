<p><p>The pipette market consists of various types tailored for specific laboratory applications. Capillary pipettes are used for precise small volume transfers; Pasteur pipettes are versatile and ideal for transferring liquids. Serological pipettes come with graduation marks for accurate measurement of larger volumes, while volumetric pipettes provide high precision for specific quantities. Piston pipettes, or micropipettes, are adjustable and widely used for precise liquid handling in various experiments. Each type caters to distinct needs in scientific research and clinical laboratories.</p></p>
