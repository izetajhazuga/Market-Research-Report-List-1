<p><p>The Passenger Vehicle Keyless Go market encompasses two main types: Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry Systems (RKES). PKES allows users to unlock and start their vehicles automatically when the key fob is in proximity, enhancing convenience and security. In contrast, RKES requires the driver to press buttons on a key fob to unlock or start the vehicle, providing a more traditional approach. Both systems aim to improve user experience and vehicle access efficiency.</p></p>
