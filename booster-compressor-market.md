<p><strong>The Booster Compressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-cooled</li><li>Water-cooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The booster compressor market is divided into two main types: air-cooled and water-cooled systems. Air-cooled compressors utilize ambient air to dissipate heat, making them suitable for installations with limited water access. They are generally easier to maintain and more energy-efficient in moderate climates. In contrast, water-cooled compressors use water for cooling, providing better thermal efficiency and performance in high-capacity applications. They are ideal for environments with consistent water supply, offering enhanced cooling but requiring more complex maintenance.</p></p>
