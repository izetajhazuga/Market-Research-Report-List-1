<p><p>Suppression film capacitors are categorized into Type I and Type II based on their construction and application. Type I capacitors feature a metalized film dielectric, offering high reliability and performance in electromagnetic interference (EMI) suppression. They are commonly used in high-frequency applications. In contrast, Type II capacitors utilize thicker films and are designed for applications requiring higher voltage ratings. They provide robust performance in power electronics and industrial environments, catering to different needs for noise reduction and signal integrity.</p></p>
