<p><strong>The Parametric Probe Card Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Leakage</li><li>Ultra Low Leakage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The parametric probe card market encompasses two primary segments: low leakage and ultra low leakage probe cards. Low leakage probe cards are designed to minimize leakage current while maintaining reliable electrical performance, making them suitable for various semiconductor testing applications. In contrast, ultra low leakage probe cards offer even greater precision, significantly reducing leakage currents, and are essential for testing sensitive devices like low-power and high-density chips. Both types cater to the evolving demands of advanced semiconductor manufacturing and testing processes.</p></p>
