<p><p>An oil-filled electric heater is a type of radiator heater that uses oil as a thermal medium. It consists of a sealed reservoir filled with diathermic oil, which is heated by electrical elements. The heated oil circulates through the radiator, effectively warming the air without producing direct heat. This results in a consistent, long-lasting warmth and improved energy efficiency.</p><p>The Oil Filled Electric Heater Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.2% during the forecast period.
