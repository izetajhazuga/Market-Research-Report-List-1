<p><p>The Traction Carbon Brushes market features three primary types: Metal Graphite, Natural Graphite, and Electrochemical Graphite. Metal Graphite brushes combine metal particles with graphite, providing excellent electrical conductivity and mechanical strength, ideal for high-performance applications. Natural Graphite brushes utilize naturally sourced graphite for stability and thermal resistance. Electrochemical Graphite brushes are engineered for superior oxidation resistance and chemical stability, making them suitable for electrochemical applications. Each type serves distinct operational needs in traction systems across various industries.</p></p>
