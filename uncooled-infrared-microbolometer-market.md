<p><strong>The Uncooled Infrared Microbolometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vox Microbolometer</li><li>α-Si Microbolometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The uncooled infrared microbolometer market is primarily classified into two types: Vox microbolometers and α-Si microbolometers. Vox microbolometers utilize vanadium oxide as the sensing material, offering high sensitivity and resolution, making them suitable for various thermal imaging applications. In contrast, α-Si microbolometers employ amorphous silicon, providing a cost-effective alternative with competitive performance in less demanding applications. Both types cater to diverse sectors, including security, automotive, and industrial monitoring, driven by increasing demand for advanced thermal surveillance technologies.</p></p>
