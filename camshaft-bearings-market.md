<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Camshaft Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Over-Head Cam (SOHC) Bearings</li><li>Dual Over-Head Cam (DOHC) Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The camshaft bearings market is segmented into Single Over-Head Cam (SOHC) and Dual Over-Head Cam (DOHC) bearings. SOHC bearings feature a single camshaft operating both intake and exhaust valves, offering a simpler design and cost-effectiveness, making them popular in smaller engines. In contrast, DOHC bearings use two camshafts for each cylinder head, allowing for better performance, higher RPMs, and improved efficiency. This results in a growing demand in high-performance and luxury vehicles, catering to diverse automotive needs.</p></p>
