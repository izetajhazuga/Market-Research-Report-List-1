<p><p>The forklift battery market includes three main types: lead-acid, nickel-based, and lithium-based batteries. Lead-acid batteries are the most traditional, known for their affordability and reliability, but they require maintenance. Nickel-based batteries offer better energy density and discharge rates but are heavier and more expensive. Lithium-based batteries are gaining popularity due to their longer lifespan, faster charging times, and reduced maintenance needs, making them ideal for modern operations focused on efficiency and sustainability in material handling.</p></p>
