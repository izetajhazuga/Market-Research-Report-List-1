<p><ul><li>Intraoral Camera</li><li>Extraoral Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital dental cameras market comprises two main types: intraoral and extraoral cameras. Intraoral cameras are small, handheld devices designed to capture detailed images inside a patient's mouth, enhancing diagnostics and patient communication. They help dentists visualize areas that are difficult to see, improving treatment planning. Extraoral cameras, on the other hand, are larger systems that capture images of the patient's face, head, and neck, aiding in orthodontic planning and overall facial assessments. Both types significantly enhance the quality of dental care.</p></p>
