<p><p>The commercial aircraft autopilot system market is categorized into several types based on complexity and functionality. Single-axis autopilot controls one axis, usually the roll, enhancing stability. Two-axis autopilot manages pitch and roll, providing better altitude and directional control. Three-axis autopilot incorporates roll, pitch, and yaw control for comprehensive navigation and handling. Additionally, other types may include advanced systems featuring GPS integration and auto-land capabilities, catering to varying levels of automation and pilot assistance in modern aviation.</p></p>
