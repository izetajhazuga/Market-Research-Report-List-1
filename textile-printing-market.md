<p><p>The textile printing market comprises various techniques, including direct printing and resist printing. Direct printing involves applying color directly onto the fabric surface, allowing for intricate designs and vibrant colors, suitable for both small and large production runs. In contrast, resist printing is a method where certain areas of the fabric are treated to prevent dye absorption, creating patterns through contrast. Both methods cater to diverse consumer preferences, driving innovation and sustainability in the textile industry.</p></p>
