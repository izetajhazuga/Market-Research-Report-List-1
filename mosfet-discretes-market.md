<p><p>The MOSFET discretes market is segmented into low-voltage (LV), medium-voltage (MV), and high-voltage (HV) MOSFETs. LV MOSFETs typically operate under 30V, suitable for applications like consumer electronics and power management. MV MOSFETs, operating between 30V to 150V, find use in automotive and industrial applications. HV MOSFETs, exceeding 150V, cater to applications in power supplies and renewable energy systems. Each type serves distinct market needs, balancing performance, efficiency, and voltage requirements across various sectors.</p></p>
