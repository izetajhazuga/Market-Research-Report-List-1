<p><strong>The Three Phase Pelletizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Twin-Screw</li><li>Single-Screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The three-phase pelletizers market is categorized into twin-screw and single-screw types. Twin-screw pelletizers utilize two intermeshing screws to enhance mixing and processing, making them ideal for complex materials and high-output production. Single-screw pelletizers, featuring a single screw for material transport, are simpler and cost-effective, suitable for less demanding applications. Both types serve distinct industries, with twin-screw offering advanced capabilities for specialized formulations, while single-screw focuses on efficiency and lower operational costs for standard materials.</p></p>
