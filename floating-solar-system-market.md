<p><p>The floating solar system market comprises various segments including PV modules, floating bodies, anchoring systems, inverters, and others. PV modules are the primary components that convert sunlight into electricity. Floating bodies provide buoyancy and support for the modules on water surfaces. Anchoring systems secure the floating structure to prevent movement and maintain stability. Inverters convert the generated DC electricity into usable AC electricity. Other market components may include monitoring systems and maintenance services, enhancing overall efficiency and performance.</p></p>
