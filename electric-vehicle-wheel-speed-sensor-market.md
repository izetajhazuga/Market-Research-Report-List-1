<p><strong>The Electric Vehicle Wheel Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Wheel Speed Sensor market is primarily divided into two types: Hall Effect sensors and Magnetic Electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors utilize magnetic fields to detect wheel rotation, providing accurate speed readings essential for vehicle stability and traction control. In contrast, Magnetic Electric sensors rely on mechanical interaction with a rotating element to generate electrical signals corresponding to speed. Both types are critical for enhancing the safety and performance of electric vehicles by enabling effective monitoring and control of wheel dynamics.</p></p>
