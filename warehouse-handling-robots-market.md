<p><p>The warehouse handling robots market consists of three main types: pick robots, sort robots, and palletize robots. Pick robots are designed to autonomously select and retrieve items from shelves, enhancing order fulfillment efficiency. Sort robots automate the organization of products, directing them to designated areas based on specified criteria, which streamlines the sorting process. Palletize robots focus on stacking products onto pallets for storage or shipment, optimizing space utilization and improving loading efficiency in warehouses.</p></p>
