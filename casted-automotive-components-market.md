<p><strong>The Casted Automotive Components Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Squeeze Casting</li><li>Investment Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The casted automotive components market includes two primary types: squeeze casting and investment casting. Squeeze casting involves forcing molten metal into a mold under high pressure, resulting in strong, precision components ideal for structural applications. Conversely, investment casting, or lost-wax casting, uses patterns coated in a ceramic shell to produce intricate shapes with excellent surface finish and dimensional accuracy. Both methods are crucial in automotive manufacturing, offering unique advantages tailored to specific component requirements, from strength to design complexity.</p></p>
